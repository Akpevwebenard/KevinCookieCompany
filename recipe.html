    chocolate chip cookie recipe

    Ingredients

  • cup butter or magarin, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 1/4cups flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup walnut or pecons, chossed(optional)
  • 2 cups xcc bitter sweet 60% cocoa baking chips

Baking Insytructions

  1. heat over to 376'C.
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. mix on low speed
  5. gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture. stire
  6. drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. bake for 9 to 11 or until chocolate cook.
  8. enjoy
